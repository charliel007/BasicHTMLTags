Change Flat Tire

  1. Get a flat and pull over
  2. Get jack, lug wrench and spare out of vehicle
  3. Loosen lug nuts
  4. Jack car up
  5. Finish removing lug nuts and remove tire
  6. Put spare on
  7. Put lug nuts on and tighten
  8. Lower vehicle down and remove jack
  9. Drive away
